# Email Cleanup Preferences

## DELETE immediately
- Delivery confirmations ("Spedizione consegnata", "order delivered")
- Pickup reminders after pickup ("Spedizione da ritirare")
- Shipping status updates ("in arrivo", "tracking ready", "transferred to")
- Google Security alerts (after review)
- Newsletters (Orange Flex, political, etc.)
- Bank policy updates (mBank, etc.)
- Vinted spam (promotions, "scontato", "Non perdere", seller messages, "Ãˆ tutto a posto?")
- Temu promotional emails

## KEEP
- Google Scholar alerts (Recommended articles)
- Order confirmations/receipts (Temu, Vinted "Aggiornamento ordine")
- Job opportunities
- Refund notifications (Klarna, etc.)
- Work-related emails
- Fresh pickup notifications (< 24h)

## ARCHIVE (not delete)
- Order confirmations after delivery complete
- Receipts for purchases
